Monocle Web Installer

Install Monocle to a LILYGO ESP32 T-Display board in your browser.

No need to mess around with commandline tools.

  1. Plug in your LILYGO ESP32 T-Display into a USB port. This tool will install Monocle to it. Where do I buy one?
  2. Hit "Install" and select the correct COM port. No device found?
  3. Monocle will take about a minute to install.
